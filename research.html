Research Work

My current research revolves around Multi-Robot Pursuit Evasion, where multiple robotic agents coordinate to detect or capture moving evaders in complex environments.

I’m particularly focused on algorithmic visibility analysis, graph-based motion planning, and real-time coordination strategies under limited communication and sensing.
