About Me

Hello! I’m Mukilan, a Master’s graduate from the University of Dayton, where I conducted research in Multi-Robot Visibility-Based Pursuit–Evasion. I received my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Engineering from SSN College of Engineering, Chennai. I am broadly interested in robotics, with previous work focusing on path planning, multi-robot systems, and related areas in robotic perception and control. I am always excited to explore new and challenging problems in robotics and intelligent systems. I have been fortunate to work with an amazing set of researchers and mentors. I am very grateful to have been advised by Dr. Nick Stiffler and to have had the opportunity to collaborate with many great people during my research journey.

Education

  • Master’s in Computer Science — University of Dayton
  • Bachelor’s in Computer Science and Engineering — SSN College of Engineering, Chennai.

Research Interests

Multi-robot systems, Pursuit-Evasion, Motion planning, Visibility-Based algorithms, and AI for robotic decision-making.
